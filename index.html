Covid - Citywide Information

MCL activities in wake of COVID-19

  • Revivial of Fire Brigade and its utilization as epidemic fighters
  • Removal of carpets from all Masajid
  • Announcement regarding epidemic at Masajid
  • Marking and regular visit to ensure social distance at masajid and business places
  • Establishment and facilitation at Quarantine Centers
  • Ensure burial of dead bodies with SOPs
  • Public awarness through banners, streamers and pamphlets
  • Establishment of Ehsaas Kafalat Centers for ease of public
